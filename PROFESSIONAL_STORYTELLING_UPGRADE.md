## Age-Appropriate Language

### Ages 3-5:
- **Vocabulary:** Simple, common words
- **Sentences:** Short (5-8 words)
- **Concepts:** Concrete, visible things
- **Example:** "Batu saw a big tree. It had a door! He opened it."

### Ages 6-8:
- **Vocabulary:** Richer, descriptive words
- **Sentences:** Medium (8-12 words)
- **Concepts:** Abstract ideas (bravery, friendship)
- **Example:** "Batu discovered an ancient oak tree with mysterious carvings. Behind it, a hidden door shimmered with golden light."

### Ages 9-12:
- **Vocabulary:** Advanced, nuanced
- **Sentences:** Longer, complex
- **Concepts:** Deeper themes (identity, purpose)
- **Example:** "As Batu approached the towering oak, its gnarled bark seemed to whisper secrets of forgotten adventures, and the door that materialized before him pulsed with an otherworldly luminescence."
